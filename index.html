Small Business Government Access Small Business Government Access (SBGSA) is a small consulting firm dedicated to helping businesses navigate the complex world of government contracting and compliance. SBGSA provides the most valuable, up-to-date information and guidance to ensure clients are well-equipped to access government opportunities, stay compliant, and grow successfully in the public sector. With personalized consulting and a deep understanding of regulatory requirements, SBGSA empowers small businesses to compete with confidence. Their commitment to client success makes them a trusted partner in achieving government contracting goals.

Who do we support?
  • Small businesses
  • Small, disadvantaged businesses
  • Women-owned small businesses
  • HUBZone small businesses
  • Veteran-owned small businesses
  • Service-disabled veteran-owned small businesses
  • Tribal small businesses
  • Alaskan native small businesses
  • Disability owned small businesses

Proposal Writing

Crafting a strong proposal is one of the most critical steps in winning a government contract. This article provides an in-depth look at how small businesses can develop clear, compelling, and compliant proposals that meet government requirements. It covers essential components such as understanding the solicitation, aligning your capabilities with the contract scope, pricing competitively, and showcasing past performance. You'll also learn how to avoid common mistakes, structure your response professionally, and tailor your proposal to highlight your unique value. Whether you're new to federal contracting or looking to improve your win rate, this guide helps you create proposals that stand out.
